Simple Luxuries presents all Slovenian regions and their nature, society, culture and tourist potential to the wider world of tourists visiting Slovenia. It involves the local population and creates a huge micro-economic movement, where everyone is empowered to act, while at the same time it helps to benefit all participating stakeholders. Simple Luxuries is creating a platform serving a particular niche in the market, where a tourist lack easy access to unique local ('five-star') experiences. Five star experience is not defined just based on the monetary value, but the intrinsic and fulfilling strife that is derived from the experience.
